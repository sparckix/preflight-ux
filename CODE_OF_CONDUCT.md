# Code Of Conduct

Preflight UX is a research-adjacent product toolkit. Contributions should be
specific, evidence-oriented, and respectful.

Contributors are expected to:

- critique ideas, methods, artifacts, and evidence rather than people
- distinguish model output from real user evidence
- avoid stereotypes and unsupported assumptions in personas or benchmark records
- document uncertainty, limitations, misses, and false positives
- treat accessibility, inclusion, and research ethics as core quality concerns
- handle sensitive product and research material carefully

Maintainers may edit, close, or reject contributions that:

- make unsupported claims
- present synthetic output as real user research
- include private or permissionless data
- introduce harmful stereotypes
- use benchmark records for broad claims that the evidence does not support
- create an unsafe or disrespectful environment for contributors

For sensitive concerns, contact the maintainers privately through the contact
path listed by the project owner. Do not post private data, credentials, or
vulnerability details in public issues.
