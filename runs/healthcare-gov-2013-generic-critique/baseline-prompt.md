## Product Surface

# Launch Surface

The launch flow required many users to create an account before browsing
available insurance plans. Account creation required multiple backend systems
and identity/security steps before users could inspect options.

Reported launch-surface characteristics:

- users had to register before browsing plans in many cases
- account creation was one of the first steps
- users encountered long waits, blank screens, error messages, and invalid
  account states
- the browsing-without-registering path was added or improved after launch
- the product served a high-stakes public-service enrollment deadline

The benchmark should be run as a public-service marketplace onboarding case.
