## Product Surface

# Launch Surface

Users attempting to access participating government services were routed through
GOV.UK Verify to prove identity with certified companies. The flow required
users to select a provider, supply identity information, and complete
verification before returning to the government service.

Reported surface characteristics:

- Verify mediated access to downstream public services
- users could fail during provider selection or identity proofing
- completion and service-access rates were below original expectations
- vulnerable or benefits-related users were among those affected
- the service was eventually closed after low adoption and performance concerns

The benchmark should be run as a high-stakes identity and public-service access
case.
